ModAhead

ModAhead is for Computer Science students in National University of Singapore (NUS) who want to plan their modules ahead of time need to use a combination of Excel sheet and referral to the NUS Computer Science course curriculum page in order to do so.

Sites such as NUSMods only offer the planning of modules for the semesters in the current academic year. It also does not provide a way for students to check which modules they need to take to complete their degree requirements. The What-if report on Edurec allows students to test and see if their plan can allow them to graduate. However, the user interface is not easy to utilise, and the user needs to regenerate their plan whenever they reuse this function.

Through the design of ModAhead, we hope to improve the experience of students that are doing long term planning of their module schedules so that they can be rest assured that they are on track towards graduation.
