ModAhead

ModAhead is for Computer Science students in National University of Singapore (NUS) who want to plan their modules ahead of time. The current workflow require students to use a combination of Excel sheet, NUSMods, What-if Report and the NUS Computer Science course curriculum page in order to do so.

Through the design of ModAhead, we hope to improve the experience of students that are doing long term planning of their module schedules so that they can be rest assured that they are on track towards graduation.
